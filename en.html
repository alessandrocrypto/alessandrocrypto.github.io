What We Build

  • Grid Engine (LIVE) — windowed grid engine for Bybit (ETHUSDT perp): TP ladder, CSV logging, checkpoint/resume and fault tolerance.
  • Grid Remote — Telegram control panel (pause/resume/status), soft-pause through KILL_SWITCH.
  • Options Services — Max Pain, Expected Move (1σ), levels/walls, option chain snapshots and analytical dashboards.
  • Strategies — grid as an execution engine plus option overlays/hedging. Principle: the grid does not “lock in losses”; risk is handled by a separate layer.
  • Volatility Analyzer — daily and multi-day volatility analysis, thresholds and Telegram notifications.
  • Impulse Detector — consolidation → volume expansion → breakout (15m / 1h), liquidity filters and perp markets.
  • Pattern Scanner — trend → volume → impulse → pattern detection with visual output.
  • Education — articles, lectures, bots, languages and market psychology.

All components are parts of one architecture: data → signals → execution → risk → dashboard.

Principles

1) No profit fairy tales. Process control comes first.
2) The grid is an execution engine and alpha collector; it must not close losses emotionally.
3) Tail risk belongs to a separate layer (overlay / hedge / options structures).
4) Reliability matters: logging, recovery, observability and control.
